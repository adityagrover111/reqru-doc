ReQru Recruitment Platform

Your innovative recruitment solution built to streamline, analyze, and enhance the entire hiring process. At ReQru, we understand that modern recruitment is more than just filling positions; it’s about finding the right talent quickly, ensuring productivity, and delivering insightful analytics for strategic growth. Our platform provides a seamless interface for tracking productivity, managing requirements, and monitoring closures, all designed to empower teams with actionable insights.

With ReQru, organizations can achieve faster hiring cycles, data-driven decision-making, and an unparalleled user experience, making talent acquisition more efficient and insightful. Join us as we redefine recruitment and help you build a workforce that drives success.

1. Dashboard

The Dashboard serves as the central hub for users upon login, providing quick insights and actionable summaries. It displays high-level metrics such as the total number of job openings, active candidates, scheduled interviews, and recent activity logs.

Users can customize widgets to focus on the information most relevant to their role, enabling them to monitor key performance indicators at a glance.

2. Skills Management

The Skills page enables administrators and recruiters to manage the catalog of skills relevant to job postings. Skills can be added, edited, or removed to ensure the database reflects current market and organizational requirements.

Skill entries support clean categorization and can be linked to multiple job roles to facilitate accurate candidate matching.

How to add a new skill:

  1. Click the 'Create New' button.
  2. Enter the skill name in the provided field.
  3. Optionally add more skills using the '+' button.
  4. Click 'Save' to finalize additions.

3. Client Management

7. Candidate Database & Onboarding

The Candidate Database maintains a comprehensive record of all candidates who have applied or been sourced. It supports search, filtering, and editing of candidate profiles.

Onboarding functionality helps transition selected candidates smoothly into the organization by tracking necessary steps and documentation.

Features:

  • Skill-Based Filtering – Quickly filter candidates by entering skills (e.g., PHP, Java) to focus on relevant profiles.
  • Search Functionality – Real-time search box allows instant location of candidates by name, email, phone number, or city.
  • Export Capability – "Export to Excel" button enables downloading the entire candidate list for offline analysis, sharing, or record-keeping.

8. Interview Schedule

The Interview Schedule enables recruiters and hiring managers to plan, edit, and cancel interviews. It includes calendar views for easy visualization and notification systems to remind participants of upcoming interviews.

Features:

  • Calendar View – Displays interviews in monthly, weekly, or daily format to help visually track interview dates.
  • Table View Toggle – Allows switching to a tabular format for a detailed list of interviews.
  • Add Interview Schedule – Opens a form with required fields: Candidate, Employee, Schedule Date, and Schedule Time; includes an optional Comment field.
  • Upcoming Interview Details – Right panel shows next scheduled interview with Candidate name, Interviewer, Date and Time, and any notes or comments.
  • Interactive Buttons – Includes Submit (save), Close (cancel), and navigation arrows for changing calendar months.

9. Team Management

Manage user accounts and roles within the ReQru platform. Assign permissions and roles such as admin, recruiter, or client user to control access and functionality.

Features:

The Team page allows efficient management of users by displaying their details such as name, email, and role in a sortable table. It includes role assignment via dropdowns, search and filter options, and action buttons to edit user roles and other details or delete users. A “Create New” button enables quick addition of team members, streamlining team administration.

10. Reports

Generate and export reports covering clients, candidates, job openings, and recruitment activities. Reports can be filtered to gain insights and improve decision-making.

Features:

  • Dashboard displaying total job applications, jobs posted, candidates hired, and interviews scheduled
  • Filter options by company, author, and date range
  • Detailed job application status table across all recruitment stages
  • Visual breakdown of candidate progress per job title
  • Easy tracking of bottlenecks in the hiring process

11. Performance Report

Detailed analytics track recruitment performance metrics including application volumes, hiring success rates, and individual recruiter activity. This helps in optimizing recruitment strategies.

Features:

  • Month-wise, recruiter-wise, and status-wise performance breakdown
  • Tabular view of reports specific to each client
  • Sorting and filtering by client name and other parameters
  • Clear visualization of recruiter contributions and hiring progress
  • Enhanced decision-making through comparative performance analysis
